Description

This dish is called Arroz con Leche (Mexican Rice Pudding). It is cooked rice with milk and cinnamon, and it makes for a very delicious desert. It is fairly simple to make and can be made in big batches.

Ingredients

  • 7 cups water
  • 1 cup of long-grain white rice
  • One 4-inch cinnamon stick
  • One 12-ounce can of evaporated milk
  • One 14-ounce can of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup golden raisins
  • Ground cinnamon for topping

Steps for the Arroz con Leche

  1. Put the water, rice, and cinnamon stick in a medium-size saucepan and set over medium-high heat
  2. Bring to a boil and cook until the rice is tender, about 18 minutes
  3. Strain out the liquid, discard the cinnamon, and reserve the rice
  4. Stir in the evaporated milk, condensed milk, and whole milk
  5. Continue cooking over medium-high head until it comes to a boil
  6. Reduce the heat to low and cook, uncovered, stirring constantly, until the mixture is thick, about 20 minutes
  7. Add the raisins, and stir well
  8. Transfer the pudding to a serving bowl and dust the top of the pudding with the ground cinnamon
